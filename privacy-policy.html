Privacy Policy for Block Drop

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

Block Drop is a simple puzzle game that:

  • Stores your high score locally on your device
  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not require internet connection to play
  • Does not share any data with third parties

Data Storage

The app only stores:

  • Your highest game score (stored locally on your device)
  • Game settings like theme preference (stored locally)

Third-Party Services

Block Drop does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Block Drop is designed for children and families. We do not collect any personal information from users of any age.
